About Us

We specialize in providing a curated selection of vintage consoles, classic video games, and accessories that transport you back to the golden age of gaming.
Our passion for preserving gaming history drives us to offer a diverse range of products and services to cater to both seasoned collectors and newcomers to the retro gaming scene.

Our Products:


1. Retro Consoles: Discover a handpicked collection of authentic and refurbished retro gaming consoles, including iconic systems like NES, SNES, Sega Genesis, and more. Each console is meticulously inspected and restored to ensure a gaming experience that stays true to its original charm.

2. Retro Video Games: Browse through our extensive library of classic video games, featuring beloved titles from franchises like Super Mario Bros., Sonic the Hedgehog, The Legend of Zelda, and many others. Whether you prefer physical cartridges or digital formats for emulation, we have something for every retro gamer.

3. Accessories and Peripherals: Enhance your gaming setup with our selection of retro accessories and peripherals. From controllers to light guns, memory cards, and more, we have the essential add-ons to complete your vintage gaming experience.

5. Community Engagement: Join our thriving community of retro gaming enthusiasts. Share your experiences, recommendations, and engage in discussions on our forums. Connect with like-minded individuals who share your passion for the classics.

6. Educational Content: Explore our informative articles, guides, and tutorials designed to assist both beginners and seasoned collectors. Learn about setting up and maintaining vintage gaming hardware, troubleshooting tips, and discover rare and sought-after retro games.

Secure Shopping Experience: At Retro City, we prioritize your security. Our website provides a safe and secure platform for online transactions, allowing you to shop with confidence and peace of mind.

Retro City is more than just a store; it's a nostalgic journey into the heart of gaming history. Join us in preserving the magic of retro gaming!
